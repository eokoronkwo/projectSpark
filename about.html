About

Trivia Champ is the ultimate site for a wide range of trivia questions from several categories.

The site can be used competitively amongst small or large groups, or by individuals just looking to learn.

Each category generates a question from a range of difficulty levels from easy to hard, whilst the random icon
on the left side of the page generates a random question from a random category from a range of difficulty
levels from easy to hard.

Good luck!
